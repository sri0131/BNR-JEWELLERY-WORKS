About

NEELAKANTESWARA RAO S/o Vishwanath chari is a renowned goldsmith who started his jewellery business in 1980. He learned the art of jewellery making from his father, who was also a skilled craftsman. He specializes in crafting exquisite pieces of jewellery using gold and silver metals, which he sources from reliable and ethical suppliers. He has a loyal customer base who appreciate his skill and quality, as well as his honesty and transparency. He is also a pioneer in introducing new designs and trends in the jewellery market, such as fusion jewellery, temple jewellery, and antique jewellery. NEELAKANTESWARA RAO's jewellery works are a symbol of elegance and excellence, and he strives to maintain his reputation and legacy.
